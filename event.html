Open Event

Artist Alley Artists/Artisans with skills ranging from amateur to professional selling comic, accessories, wallpaper, and a variety of other merch to display. We accept all styles of comic (American Comic, Japanese Manga, Korean Manhwa, etc.), and welcome anyone who is interested in applying to be a part of Artist Alley.
Arcade Land Feel like taking a break? Enjoy some old fashion arcade games like Metal Slug, Pac-Man, Time Crisis, House of the Dead, Street Fighter, Tetris, Puzzle Bobble, and more.

Scheduled Event

Event Day Hours
Comic 101 Friday, Saturday, Sunday 3:00pm - 6:00pm
Startup Workshop Friday, Saturday, Sunday 5:00pm - 8:00pm
Rising Talent Promotion Friday, Saturday, Sunday 6:00pm - 9:00pm

Comic 101: To help new talent gain the courage to take the first step into being a comic creator, we have invited a panel of creators with diverse level of experience and skills, and have them share where their journey began and how they made their dreams come true.

Startup Workshop: Don't know where to start? We'll show you the way! With our Startup Workshop, we will teach you the arts and craft of basic story structure, character design, printing process, and how to pitch and sell your creativity to the world.

Rising Talent Promotion: New talents in the world of comic will be given an opportunity to have their work displayed and promoted to everyone attending our event. We will be promoting your name and your works to every backer and supporter we can appeal to and bring you great success in your endeavor as a comic creator.
