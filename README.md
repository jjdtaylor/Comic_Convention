# Indie Comic Convention

The Indie Comic Convention website that advertises a comic convention that welcome everyone of all ages, and to have fun with friends and family, while also learning about the world of comic and the wonderful stories that comic creators can create. The goal of this website and this convention, is to inspire and give birth to new talents who would want to have the stories that they would want to create and share to the world.


## Webpages purpose

Home (index.html), welcomes visitors to the website and gives a brief overview on the themes, activities, and goal.

Event (event.html), shows every activity that the convention have to offer, along with the event's schedule for attendee's convenience.

Volunteer (volunteer.html), allows people the chance to sign up and be a part of the convention. 


## Website's General Functionality

The website has a total of three webpages that can accessed by the links in the navigation bar: Home, Event, Volunteer. The three links can also be found at the bottom of the page, in the footer section, along with links to a few social media sites (Note: The links to social media sites are not anchored to anything. Basically, clicking them won't do anything).

To make things more convenient, a "back-to-top" button was added to the bottom-right corner of web pages that can immediately take you to the top of the page without scrolling.
