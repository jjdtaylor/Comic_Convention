About Us

We are a brand new convention that will make our debut in the year 2027, our sole reason for existing is to find and introduce new talents into the world of comics and give them a place to create connections, build portfolios, and stand out in the competitive market of the comic industry. We accept and invite all kinds of comic from American comic, Japanese manga, Chinese manhua, Korean manhwa, and more. Whether you're an amateur or a professional; everyone is welcome to join us as we aspire for all comic creator to elevate each other and create a space for next generation of comic creators to prosper, while upholding the spirit and purpose of Indie Comic Convention for the following years to come.
