Policy

*** We run a family-friendly event for all ages and do not condone or promote content that would be visually gory, sexually explicit, and hyper violent. Attendee who violate of this policy will be removed and barred from convention.

Attendee

After registration is submitted and approved, you will officially be invited as an anttendee of the Indie Comic Convention. Attendees of the event are held to a standard of fulfilling their assigned duties and responsibilites during convention hours and will be compensated for their time and effort after the entire convention is concluded.

Cancellation

If an applicant cannot participate in the Indie Comic Convention under any circumstances, please contact us via email or contact form below as soon as possible so that we may cancel your application and provide an open slot to the next applicant. No fines will be imposed as long as cancellation was made 60 days before the start of the convention.

Rules of Conduct

Our objective for the Indie Comic Convention is to provide a friendly, safe, and welcoming environment for everyone attending our event. We want everyone to have a wonderful experience and to not feel discouraged from enjoying everything all of our events have to offer. We believe that everyone who attend our convention should be treated with upmost respect and courtesy. Any potential danger that might occur in sight must be reported to the nearest security staff, so that the threat can be handled immediately and prevent further escalation.
